The mediator pattern is a behavioral design pattern that centralizes complex
communications between the objects into a single mediation object.

Instead of objects communicating between them directly talking to each other,
rather they communicate via the mediator class, which is how the other users
comes to know about the updates.

Example:
1. Principal in the school every communication goes via the principal

Without mediator:
Teacher <----> Student
Teacher <----> Accountant
Student <----> Principal
Accountant <----> Principal

Everybody talks to everybody.

This creates:

tight coupling
too many dependencies
hard maintenance
chaotic communication

With mediator:

Teacher -----\
Student ------> Principal (Mediator)
Accountant --/

Nobody talks directly.

Everyone talks only to the mediator.

2. WhatsApp Group
When we send the messages tagging others but under the hood we are
sending it to the WhatsApp servers, which is basically a mediator.
